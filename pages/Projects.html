There are a few factors influencing delays in communication links. In an ELO project, our lecturer tasked us to find ways to minimise nodal processing delays in switches (hubs) and I used Mininet emulation tool to experiment on the challenge. The end goal was to increase the physical distance as possible for a given Return-Trip-Time (RTT) by reducing processing delays.

Communication Link Delays
